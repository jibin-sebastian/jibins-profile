    About Me

    I'm a passionate Data Scientist with a penchant for solving real-world challenges and a knack for staying abreast of the latest trends in data science.

Prior to this, I gained valuable experience as a working student at Creditshelf AG, focusing on data management, analysis, and API development.

    DevOps Experience:
    My journey in technology began as a Systems Engineer at Infosys Pvt Ltd, where I honed my skills in incident management, change management, and Kubernetes orchestration. I've cultivated expertise in Unix and Windows administration, SLA management, and monitoring tools like Splunk and AppDynamics.

    Education & Projects:
    I hold an MSc in Data Science and have undertaken various projects, including flight fare prediction in the aviation domain, unsupervised machine learning for customer segmentation in retail, and ongoing research on activity group classification using empirical motion data.

    Certifications & Skills:
    Alongside my academic pursuits, I've earned certifications in Python and SQL, and I'm currently expanding my expertise in TensorFlow and Keras frameworks. My toolkit includes Python, Panda, NumPy, Matplotlib, Flask, PLSQL, Statistics, and a deep understanding of machine learning algorithms.

    Conclusion:
    I'm dedicated to leveraging data science and automation to simplify lives and drive innovation. With a passion for learning and a commitment to excellence, I look forward to contributing meaningfully to future endeavors.
